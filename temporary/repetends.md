# Haxophone
Small travel digital saxophone under $100 that provides a satisfactory user experience. Features the same fingering as a real saxophone.
The hardware is a HAT (Hardware attached on top) product for a Raspberry Pi single board computer. The software is free to use.
https://github.com/cardonabits/haxo-hw
https://github.com/cardonabits/haxo-rs
https://www.crowdsupply.com/cardona-bits/haxophone
The haxophone isn’t intended to replace the saxophone → it’s simply for scenarios where it is inconvenient to bring or set up a real saxophone.
https://www.crowdsupply.com/cardona-bits/haxophone

# 8. XRP - Short for the “Experiential Robotics Platform”
Uses Raspberry Pi Pico W microcontroller
https://experientialrobotics.org/
The XRP is a compact, cost-effective robotic platform designed to provide students, educators, and teams with an authentic and rigorous introduction to robotics programming. 
It was developed through a collaboration between WPI and DEKA Research & Development Corp. It aims to deliver an experiential learning experience that is both comprehensive and accessible to help level the STEM playing field globally and create a future generation of STEM innovators and technology leaders.
